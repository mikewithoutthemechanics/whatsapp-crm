# Pricing Plans

## Starter (Free)
- **Price**: $0/month (ZAR 0)
- **Features**:
  - AI Auto-Reply (14,400 requests/day via Groq)
  - Lead Scoring (basic)
  - Drip Campaigns (limited)
  - OpenWA integration
  - Community support
- **Best for**: Testing, freelancers, very small businesses

## Professional
- **Price**: $199/month (ZAR 3,500)
- **Features**:
  - Unlimited AI requests
  - Multi-language support (EN, AF, ZU, XH)
  - Custom branding (logo, colors)
  - Priority email support
  - Advanced lead scoring
  - Unlimited drip campaigns
  - Broadcast messaging
  - 99.9% uptime SLA
- **Best for**: Growing SMEs, marketing agencies

## Enterprise
- **Price**: $999/month (ZAR 17,500)
- **Features**:
  - Everything in Professional
  - Dedicated server instance
  - White-label solution (remove all branding)
  - 24/7 phone support
  - Custom integrations (CRM, ERP)
  - Advanced analytics dashboard
  - API access with higher rate limits
  - On-premise deployment option
  - Custom feature development
- **Best for**: Large businesses, franchises, multi-location operations

## Add-ons
- Additional AI requests: $50 per 100k requests
- Priority support upgrade: $50/month
- Custom domain (included in Pro & Enterprise)
- Training & onboarding: $500 one-time

All prices exclude VAT where applicable. Monthly billing with annual discount available (2 months free).
